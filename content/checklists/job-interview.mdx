---
title: "Job Interview Preparation"
category: "career"
description: "Complete checklist for preparing for technical interviews"
priority: "medium"
items:
  - Pre-Interview Research
    - Research the company and role
    - Understand the team structure
    - Review company values and culture
    - Prepare thoughtful questions
  - Technical Preparation
    - Review core concepts
    - Practice coding problems
    - Prepare system design questions
    - Review past projects
  - Materials Preparation
    - Update resume and portfolio
    - Prepare references
    - Gather required documents
    - Choose appropriate attire
  - Interview Day
    - Arrive early (virtual or in-person)
    - Bring necessary materials
    - Maintain professional demeanor
    - Follow up with thank-you note
---
